Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 24, 2026

Short version: WalletConvertPlus does not collect, store, or share any personal information. All your data stays on your device.

1. Introduction

Welcome to WalletConvertPlus ("we", "our", or "us"). This Privacy Policy describes how WalletConvertPlus ("the App") handles information when you use our mobile application available on the Google Play Store.

By using the App, you agree to the terms described in this policy. If you disagree with any part of this policy, please discontinue use of the App.

2. Information We Collect

We collect no personal information. The App does not require account creation, login, or any form of identification.

The only data the App stores is:

  • Your wallet names and asset amounts (stored locally on your device using Hive)
  • Your price alarm settings (stored locally on your device)
  • Your portfolio entries (stored locally on your device)
  • Your subscription status (stored locally via SharedPreferences)

None of this data is transmitted to our servers or any third party. It exists solely on your device and is deleted when you uninstall the App.

3. Exchange Rate Data

The App fetches live exchange rate data from the following public, free-to-use APIs:

  • Binance Public API (api.binance.com) — for cryptocurrency prices. No API key or account is required. These requests contain no identifying information.
  • Frankfurter.app (api.frankfurter.app) — for fiat currency rates sourced from the European Central Bank. No API key or account is required. These requests contain no identifying information.

4. Advertising

The free tier of the App may display advertisements served by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data as described in Google's Privacy Policy . You can opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings (Settings → Privacy → Ads on Android).

Pro subscribers see no advertisements.

5. In-App Purchases

Pro subscription purchases are processed entirely by the Google Play Store. We do not receive, store, or process any payment card information. All billing is handled by Google as described in Google Payments Terms of Service .

6. Permissions

The App requests the following Android permissions:

  • INTERNET — required to fetch live exchange rates from Binance and Frankfurter APIs, and to display advertisements.
  • ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE — used to check whether an internet connection is available before making network requests, to improve offline experience.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can remove it.

8. Data Security

Since all user data is stored locally on your device, its security depends on your device's own security mechanisms (screen lock, encryption). We recommend keeping your device updated and using a strong screen lock.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
